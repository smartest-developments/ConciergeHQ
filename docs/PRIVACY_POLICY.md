# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-08

## Scope
This policy applies to Acquisition Concierge account, sourcing-request, and operational support data.

## Data We Collect
- Account identity data: email, password hash, role.
- Sourcing workflow data: prescription metadata, request states, operator/admin actions.
- Security and audit data: session metadata, immutable status events, legal/compliance traces.

## Purpose of Processing
- Deliver requested sourcing and report services.
- Operate account/session security controls and abuse prevention.
- Meet legal, compliance, and incident-response obligations.

## Data Minimization
We collect and retain only data required for service execution, security, and legal obligations. We do not process end-merchant checkout funds.

## Retention
Operational and audit records are retained according to legal/compliance and incident-response requirements. Data no longer required for those obligations is removed or anonymized.

## GDPR Rights
Authenticated users can submit:
- Data export requests.
- Deletion requests (subject to legal-hold checks and regulated retention obligations).

## International Scope
Service scope is EU + CH. Country/category restrictions are defined in `docs/LEGAL_BOUNDARIES.md`.

## Contact and Requests
Privacy and rights requests must be handled through authenticated in-product controls to ensure identity and authorization verification.
