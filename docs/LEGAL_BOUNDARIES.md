# Legal Boundaries (v1)

## Service role
Acquisition Concierge is a sourcing and research service. We are not a seller, reseller, distributor, payment processor, logistics provider, or warranty provider.

## Purchase relationship
- The final purchase contract is strictly between the user and the external merchant.
- User pays the merchant directly on the merchant platform.
- Acquisition Concierge does not collect, hold, or transmit item purchase funds.

## Non-refundable service fee
- The sourcing fee pays for research, market scan, and proposal preparation.
- The fee is non-refundable once work begins, regardless of whether the user buys the proposed item.

## Privacy policy baseline
- Data minimization: we collect only account and sourcing-request data required to deliver the service.
- Purpose limitation: personal data is used for request execution, support, and security/compliance operations.
- GDPR rights: authenticated users can submit export/deletion requests through in-product controls.
- Retention: operational/audit records are retained for legal obligations and incident response.

## Terms of service baseline
- The platform provides informational sourcing output only.
- Final pricing, inventory, shipping, and after-sales obligations remain with the external merchant.
- Users are responsible for customs/import obligations when applicable.
- Refund handling follows the non-refundable sourcing-fee policy above and explicit case-by-case legal review where required.

## After-sales exclusion
Acquisition Concierge does not provide:
- Returns processing
- Warranty claims
- Repairs
- Post-delivery dispute handling
- Ongoing support with merchant fulfillment

## Category and jurisdiction restrictions
- Supported geographies: EU member states + Switzerland (CH).
- Supported categories: Electronics, Home Appliances, Sports Equipment.
- Explicitly prohibited: cars, real estate, medicines, weapons, illegal items.

## Proposal validity
- Proposal links are time-bound and expire 2 hours after publication.
- Availability and merchant pricing can change after expiry.
